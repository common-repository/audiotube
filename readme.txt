== Description ==

AudioTube is a WordPress plugin that lets you embed YouTube videos simply as audio players. It’s great for adding songs or spoken word to your posts and pages.

See more info at http://davidangel.net/audiotube/


== Installation ==

How to Use:

*   Upload/install the plugin
*   On your post or page click on the AudioTube button in the visual editor (it's black with a white musical note)
*   Paste in the YouTube video on prompt
*   Optionally type a caption
*   Publish or update your post
